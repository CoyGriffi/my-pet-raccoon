Jerry can very often be a handfull, and to ensure a safe encounter you will want to make sure to follow these strict guidelines.

Rules of encounter

  1. Approach SLOWLY!
  2. Always approach at Raccoon height as not to intimidate him.
  3. Bring a treat as an offer of appeasment
  4. hand treat to Jerry with palms flat and open so he knows you don't have a weapon

Failure to follow any of these rules could result in SEVERE Injury
